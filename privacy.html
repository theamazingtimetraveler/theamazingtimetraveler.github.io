Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

1. Introduction

The Amazing Time Traveler ("we," "our," or "Service") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our content scheduling and publishing tool.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Authorization Data: When you connect your TikTok account, we receive an access token and user ID through TikTok's OAuth authentication
  • Content: Videos, images, captions, and metadata you upload for publishing

2.2 Information from Third Parties

  • TikTok Account Information: Basic profile information (username, user ID) necessary to post content on your behalf
  • Platform Tokens: Authentication tokens from connected platforms (TikTok, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube)

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Scheduling times, posting history, and feature usage
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device information, and IP address for security purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Publish content to your connected social media accounts at scheduled times
  • Maintain and improve the Service
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Use your content for advertising purposes
  • Share your data with unaffiliated companies for their marketing

4. Information Sharing

We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you authorize posting to connected platforms
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help operate our tool (hosting, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • Platform APIs: Necessary data shared with TikTok, Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube to publish your content

5. Data Retention

  • Access Tokens: Stored securely while your account is connected; deleted upon disconnection
  • Published Content Metadata: Retained for your posting history; deletable upon request
  • Usage Logs: Retained for 90 days for troubleshooting purposes

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Encrypted storage of access tokens
  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Regular security updates
  • Limited access to personal data

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data
  • Disconnect: Revoke access to connected platforms at any time
  • Opt-Out: Stop using the Service at any time

8. Third-Party Platforms

When you connect TikTok, Facebook, Instagram, or YouTube:

  • Your use of those platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies
  • We only access the minimum data necessary to publish content
  • You can revoke our access through each platform's settings

Platform Privacy Policies:

  • TikTok: tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Facebook/Instagram: facebook.com/privacy/policy
  • YouTube: policies.google.com/privacy

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. International Users

If you are accessing the Service from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy with an updated "Last Updated" date.
